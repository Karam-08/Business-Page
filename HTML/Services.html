What do we do?

The Ford Company is a car manufacturing business. Our business makes a variety of automobiles, including Commercial Vehicles, Trucks, and SUVs. We also create car parts that you can buy.

We have special services for specific needs, which include Emergency Vehicles, Military Vehicles (not available to the public), and various other Specialized Vehicles. There are also discounts that we can give if you meet specific requirements. For example, if you are a Veteran or employed in a School, we will give a 15% discount.

There are also special requests that you can ask for when ordering a car (must be done in person). For example, you can:

  • Select the type of Engine you would want on your car
  • Choose what kind of Tires and Wheels you'd want on your car
  • Include a built in Entertainment System
